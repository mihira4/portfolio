EntreConnect

EntreConnect is an AI-driven networking platform built to empower entrepreneurs by connecting them with peers, mentors, and investors based on shared interests, startup stage, and domain expertise. It features intelligent matchmaking using cosine similarity, an AI-powered pitch analyzer that offers structured feedback on short video pitches, and a curated portal to engage directly with top-rated mentors and investors. The platform also includes a 24/7 AI chatbot assistant to guide users through startup essentials like funding, registration, and market research. Built with a modern tech stack including React, Node.js, Flask, and Gemini API, EntreConnect blends smart technology with real human connections to accelerate entrepreneurial growth.
